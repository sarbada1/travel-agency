Description

This modern L-shaped sectional sofa is the perfect addition to any contemporary living space. Featuring a sleek design with clean lines and plush cushions, this sofa combines style and comfort effortlessly.

The sofa is upholstered in high-quality, durable gray fabric that is both stain-resistant and easy to clean. The cushions are filled with high-density foam for maximum comfort and longevity, ensuring they maintain their shape even with regular use.

The L-shaped design includes a chaise lounge on one end, providing the perfect spot to stretch out and relax. The modular design allows for flexible arrangement to suit your space and needs.

Key features include:

  • L-shaped configuration with chaise lounge
  • Premium stain-resistant fabric upholstery
  • High-density foam cushions for superior comfort
  • Solid wood frame for durability
  • Removable and washable cushion covers
  • Modern design with clean lines
  • Sturdy metal legs with non-marking feet

This sectional sofa is brand new and comes with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty. Assembly is required, but all necessary hardware and instructions are included. The sofa ships in 3 boxes for easy handling.

Perfect for family rooms, entertainment spaces, or any living area where comfort and style are priorities. This is a floor model that has never been used and is in perfect condition.
