Description

This 2023 Tesla Model 3 Long Range AWD is in excellent condition with only 12,500 miles. The vehicle comes with the Premium Interior package, Enhanced Autopilot, and the latest software updates.

The Pearl White exterior is pristine with no scratches or dents, and the Black Premium Interior shows minimal wear. This Model 3 includes the 18" Aero Wheels and has been garage-kept since purchase.

The Long Range AWD model offers an EPA-estimated range of 358 miles on a full charge and features dual motor all-wheel drive for excellent performance in all weather conditions. The vehicle accelerates from 0-60 mph in just 4.2 seconds.

This Model 3 is still under the original factory warranty, which includes:

  • Basic Vehicle - 4 years or 50,000 miles (whichever comes first)
  • Battery & Drive Unit - 8 years or 120,000 miles (whichever comes first)

Charging accessories included: Mobile Connector, NEMA 14-50 Adapter, and J1772 Public Charging Adapter.

Clean title, non-smoker, no accidents, and all service records available. Selling due to relocation overseas.
