About AdSpot

Connecting buyers and sellers since 2010. Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind AdSpot.

Our Story

AdSpot began with a simple idea: make buying and selling easier for everyone.

Founded in 2010 by Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen, AdSpot started as a small local classifieds website serving the New York area. What began as a passion project in a tiny apartment has grown into a global marketplace platform operating in 25 countries and connecting millions of buyers and sellers every day.

Our founders recognized that traditional classified ads were becoming obsolete, but the need for local commerce was stronger than ever. They envisioned a digital platform that would make it simple for anyone to buy or sell goods and services in their community and beyond.

Our Mission

To create the most trusted and efficient marketplace where anyone can buy, sell, and connect.

We're committed to building technology that removes barriers to commerce and creates economic opportunity for individuals and businesses around the world. By providing a safe, simple platform for exchange, we help communities thrive and enable sustainable consumption.

Our Vision

A world where anyone can easily and safely transact with anyone, anywhere.

We envision a future where geographic boundaries and technological barriers no longer limit commerce. Where buying and selling is accessible to all, regardless of technical expertise or location. Where trust and transparency are the foundation of every transaction.

Our Values

Trust & Safety

We prioritize creating a secure environment where users can transact with confidence. Trust is the foundation of our marketplace.

We build for the needs of our users and the communities they create. Their success is our success.

Innovation

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth from a small startup to a global marketplace

2010

The Beginning

AdSpot Founded

Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen launch AdSpot in New York City, focusing initially on local classifieds.

2012

Expansion

National Expansion

AdSpot expands to serve all major U.S. cities and secures its first round of venture funding.

2015

Innovation

Mobile App Launch

AdSpot releases its first mobile app, reaching 5 million users and expanding to Canada and the UK.

2018

Growth

Global Expansion

AdSpot expands to 10 countries across Europe and Asia, reaching 20 million monthly active users.

2020

Adaptation

Digital Transformation

AdSpot introduces virtual viewings and contactless transactions in response to global challenges.

2023

Achievement

50 Million Users

AdSpot reaches 50 million monthly active users and launches AdSpot Pay for secure transactions.

2025

Today

Global Leader

AdSpot operates in 25 countries, with 10 new markets added in 2025, and continues to innovate with AI-powered features.
