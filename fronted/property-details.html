Description

Welcome to this stunning luxury apartment in the heart of New York City. This modern 3-bedroom, 2-bathroom residence offers breathtaking city views through floor-to-ceiling windows and has been fully renovated with high-end finishes throughout.

The open-concept living area features hardwood floors, recessed lighting, and a sleek gas fireplace. The gourmet kitchen is equipped with custom cabinetry, quartz countertops, and top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances including a wine refrigerator.

The spacious primary suite includes a walk-in closet and an en-suite bathroom with a double vanity, soaking tub, and glass-enclosed shower. Two additional well-proportioned bedrooms and a second full bathroom complete the living quarters.

Building amenities include 24-hour doorman and concierge service, a state-of-the-art fitness center, resident lounge, and a landscaped roof deck with panoramic views. Conveniently located near public transportation, fine dining, shopping, and parks.
