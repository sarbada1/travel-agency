AdSpot Expands to 10 New International Markets

March 20, 2025 | 5 min read

AdSpot, the leading online marketplace platform, announced today its expansion into 10 new international markets across Europe and Asia, bringing its total global presence to 25 countries.

The expansion includes launches in Poland, Czech Republic, Hungary, Romania, Greece, Portugal, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, and the Philippines. Each market will feature a fully localized platform tailored to regional preferences and needs.

Strategic Growth Initiative

This expansion is part of AdSpot's strategic growth initiative aimed at capturing emerging markets with high digital adoption rates and growing e-commerce sectors. The company has invested over $50 million in the expansion, which includes localization, marketing, and building local teams in each new market.

"We're thrilled to bring the AdSpot platform to these dynamic markets," said Sarah Johnson, CEO of AdSpot. "Our research shows strong demand for a comprehensive marketplace solution in these regions, and we're confident that our technology and approach will resonate with local users."

"Our mission has always been to connect buyers and sellers in the most efficient way possible, regardless of geography. These new markets represent a significant opportunity to further that mission while driving substantial business growth."

Sarah Johnson, CEO of AdSpot

Localized Approach

Each new market will feature:

  • Fully translated platforms in local languages
  • Integration with local payment methods
  • Partnerships with local delivery and logistics providers
  • Customer support teams based in each country
  • Marketing campaigns tailored to local preferences and customs

The company has hired over 200 new employees across these markets to support the expansion, with plans to double that number by the end of the year.

New AdSpot office in Bangkok, Thailand

AdSpot team in Warsaw, Poland

Market Potential

According to market research conducted by AdSpot, these 10 new markets represent a combined potential user base of over 300 million people, with e-commerce growth rates averaging 15-20% annually.

"These markets are at an inflection point in terms of digital marketplace adoption," explained Michael Chen, AdSpot's Chief Strategy Officer. "We're entering at the perfect time to establish ourselves as the platform of choice for buyers and sellers."

Future Plans

The company has indicated that this expansion is just the beginning of a broader global strategy. AdSpot plans to enter an additional 15 markets over the next three years, with a particular focus on Latin America and Africa.

"Our goal is to be present in 50 countries by 2028," said Johnson. "We believe our platform can make a meaningful difference in how people buy and sell goods and services around the world, particularly in regions where e-commerce infrastructure is still developing."

Key Expansion Facts

  • 10 new markets across Europe and Asia
  • Total investment of over $50 million
  • 200+ new employees hired locally
  • Potential user base of 300 million people
  • Full localization in all markets

The new platforms are already live in Poland, Czech Republic, and Thailand, with the remaining markets scheduled to launch by the end of April 2025.

For more information about AdSpot's international expansion or to access the new platforms, visit www.adspot.com/global.
