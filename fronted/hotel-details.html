Description

Welcome to the Grand Luxury Resort & Spa, a premier beachfront destination in Miami Beach. Our five-star resort offers an unparalleled luxury experience with breathtaking ocean views, world-class amenities, and exceptional service.

Nestled on the pristine shores of Miami Beach, our resort features elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each designed with sophisticated décor and modern amenities to ensure your utmost comfort. From private balconies overlooking the Atlantic Ocean to plush bedding and spacious bathrooms, every detail has been carefully considered.

Indulge in culinary excellence at our four distinctive restaurants, offering everything from fine dining to casual beachside fare. Our award-winning chefs create memorable dining experiences using the freshest local ingredients and international flavors.

Rejuvenate your body and mind at our luxurious spa, featuring a comprehensive menu of treatments, therapies, and wellness programs. Unwind in our steam rooms, saunas, and relaxation lounges, or maintain your fitness routine at our state-of-the-art fitness center.

For recreation, enjoy our multiple swimming pools, including an adults-only infinity pool and a family-friendly pool with water features. Our private beach offers exclusive access to pristine sands and crystal-clear waters, with attentive beach service throughout the day.

Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, family vacation, or corporate retreat, the Grand Luxury Resort & Spa provides the perfect setting for an unforgettable Miami Beach experience.
