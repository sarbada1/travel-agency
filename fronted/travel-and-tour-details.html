Tour Overview

Experience the best of Bali with this unforgettable 7-day paradise tour that showcases the island's natural beauty, rich culture, and spiritual traditions. From pristine beaches and lush rice terraces to ancient temples and vibrant local markets, this carefully crafted itinerary captures the essence of this Indonesian paradise.

Our expert local guides will take you beyond the typical tourist experience, introducing you to authentic Balinese culture, cuisine, and traditions. You'll visit iconic landmarks, participate in traditional ceremonies, and have plenty of time to relax on some of the world's most beautiful beaches.

Perfect for first-time visitors and seasoned travelers alike, our Bali Paradise Tour offers the perfect balance of adventure, cultural immersion, and relaxation. Whether you're seeking spiritual renewal, adventure activities, or simply a beautiful beach getaway, this tour offers something for everyone.

Detailed Itinerary

Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport in Denpasar, you'll be greeted by our friendly tour representative who will transfer you to your beachfront hotel in Kuta. Take some time to settle in, relax by the pool, or take a short stroll to the famous Kuta Beach to catch your first Balinese sunset.

In the evening, join the group for a welcome dinner featuring traditional Balinese cuisine at a local restaurant. Our tour guide will provide a brief orientation about the exciting week ahead while you enjoy authentic local dishes and get to know your fellow travelers.

Highlights:
  • Airport pickup and hassle-free transfer
  • First glimpse of beautiful Kuta Beach
  • Traditional Balinese welcome dinner

Dinner

After breakfast at the hotel, we'll begin our exploration of Bali's southern peninsula. First, we'll visit the stunning clifftop Uluwatu Temple (Pura Luhur Uluwatu), one of Bali's six key spiritual pillars. Perched dramatically on a steep cliff 70 meters above the Indian Ocean, this temple offers breathtaking views and is home to mischievous monkeys.

Next, we'll visit the beautiful Padang Padang Beach, a small but picturesque beach famous for its appearance in the movie "Eat, Pray, Love." Enjoy some free time to relax, swim, or explore the unique rock formations.

As the sun begins to set, we'll return to Uluwatu Temple to watch the spectacular Kecak Fire Dance performance against the backdrop of the sunset over the Indian Ocean. This mesmerizing cultural show tells the story of Ramayana through rhythmic chanting, dance, and fire.

Highlights:
  • Visit the majestic clifftop Uluwatu Temple
  • Relax at the beautiful Padang Padang Beach
  • Experience the traditional Kecak Fire Dance at sunset

Today we head to Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali. After checking into our hotel, we'll visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary, home to over 700 long-tailed macaques and three ancient temples within a lush jungle setting.

Next, we'll explore Ubud's thriving arts scene with visits to traditional art galleries and the ARMA Museum (Agung Rai Museum of Art), which houses an exceptional collection of Balinese and Indonesian paintings and sculptures.

We'll have lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the scenic rice fields, then spend time exploring the bustling Ubud Traditional Market, where you can shop for souvenirs, handcrafts, and textiles. The remainder of the afternoon is free for you to explore Ubud at your own pace or take an optional Balinese cooking class (additional cost).

Highlights:
  • Encounter playful macaques at the Ubud Monkey Forest
  • Discover Balinese art at galleries and museums
  • Shop for unique souvenirs at Ubud Traditional Market

After breakfast, we'll journey north of Ubud to the spectacular Tegalalang Rice Terraces. These iconic stepped paddies offer stunning views and photo opportunities. You'll learn about the traditional Balinese cooperative irrigation system known as "subak," which has been recognized by UNESCO.

Next, we'll visit a local coffee plantation to learn about the production of Balinese coffee, including the famous Luwak coffee. Enjoy a tasting of various coffees and teas while taking in the plantation views.

In the afternoon, we'll visit Tirta Empul Temple, a sacred water temple where Balinese Hindus have been participating in ritual purification in the holy spring water for over a thousand years. You'll have the opportunity to observe or participate in this cleansing ritual (appropriate sarongs provided). We'll end the day with a visit to the Gunung Kawi Temple, featuring 10 rock-cut shrines (candi) carved into the cliff face.

Highlights:
  • Marvel at the iconic Tegalalang Rice Terraces
  • Experience a coffee plantation and tasting
  • Visit the holy springs at Tirta Empul Temple
  • Explore the ancient Gunung Kawi rock-cut temple

Today we explore the natural wonders of Bali's interior. Early risers have the option of a pre-dawn hike up Mount Batur to witness the sunrise (additional cost). For those not taking the sunrise hike, the day begins with breakfast at the hotel before departing for the Kintamani region.

At Kintamani, we'll enjoy breathtaking views of Mount Batur, an active volcano, and the beautiful Lake Batur. We'll have lunch at a restaurant with panoramic views of the volcano and lake.

In the afternoon, we'll visit Besakih Temple, known as the "Mother Temple" of Bali. Located on the slopes of Mount Agung, this is the largest and most important temple in Balinese Hinduism, consisting of 23 separate temples. On our way back, we'll stop at Tegenungan Waterfall, where you can take a refreshing dip in the natural pool beneath the cascading waters.

Highlights:
  • Experience the stunning views of Mount Batur and Lake Batur
  • Visit Besakih, the holiest temple in Bali
  • Swim at Tegenungan Waterfall

After breakfast, we'll check out of our Ubud hotel and travel to the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, one of Bali's most important sea temples, dramatically perched on a rocky outcrop surrounded by crashing waves. This temple is particularly beautiful in the morning light when fewer tourists are present.

Next, we'll head to Nusa Dua, where we'll check into our luxury beachfront resort. The afternoon is yours to enjoy at leisure – relax on the pristine white sand beach, take a dip in the crystal-clear waters, or enjoy the resort's facilities.

In the late afternoon, indulge in a complimentary 1-hour traditional Balinese massage at the resort's spa, a perfect way to rejuvenate after days of exploration. In the evening, enjoy a delicious seafood dinner at a beachfront restaurant.

Highlights:
  • Visit the iconic Tanah Lot sea temple

  • Savor a seafood dinner by the beach

Enjoy a leisurely morning on your final day in Bali. Sleep in, have a relaxed breakfast, take a final swim in the ocean or pool, or simply soak up the tropical atmosphere.
